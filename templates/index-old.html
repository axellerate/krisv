Welcome to my little corner in the vastness of the internet!

My name is Kristoffer Vukasinovic and welcome to personal website. I am currently a Software Development continuously piecing together a greater understanding of programming languages and design. I find the digital world and its tenacious function to contantly improve to be fascinating.

Computation as well as the internet has granted us a vast array of tools that each and every one of us use on a day-to-day basis. It has given birth to a never before seen age of information transfer and automation that is almost beyond imagination. Just think about what is involved to simply view the text and images that are before your eyes. The website data stored in a server far, far way is read, packed up and transfered at the speed of light through a network that finds its way all the way to your household. It is then interpreted by your browser, contructed and shown to you - all within about a second or so. Simply amazing!
